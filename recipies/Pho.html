Traditional Pho Recipe

Pho is a Vietnamese soup dish made with broth, rice noodles, herbs, and meat or tofu.

Ingredients

  • Beef Broth
  • Yelllow Onion
  • Fresh Ginger
  • Spice Mixture

Steps

  1. Quarter the onion
  2. Add the onion and the ginger to a large pot
  3. Cood over medium heat for roughly four(4) minutes
  4. Add the broth and spice mixture
  5. Simmer for at least 30 minutes
